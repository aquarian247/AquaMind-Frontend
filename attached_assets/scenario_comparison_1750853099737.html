Recommendation

Based on the comparison, the Q3 High Efficiency scenario offers the best balance of:

  • Reduced feed costs (-$31,200 compared to standard plan)
  • Improved FCR (1.15 vs 1.20 standard)
  • Shorter production cycle (87 days vs 92 days standard)
  • Minimal impact on final biomass (only 1.1% reduction)

The accelerated growth scenario offers the fastest production time but at the expense of higher feed costs and lower survival rates.
